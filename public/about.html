Biography

Hi!
My name is Manuel Lerchner, and I am passionate about informatics, programming, and science.

Ever since my early childhood, I have been fascinated by technology and how technical devices work. This curiosity continues to drive me today, and I often lose track of everything around me when working on a project. Over the past few years, my interest has increasingly focused on computer technology, allowing me to dedicate more time to computer science in my spare time. Through this, I have gained programming experience in various languages by completing smaller projects.

I love learning, solving problems, and building things. I want to use these talents to design and create systems that benefit humanity and contribute to developing technologies that improve people's lives and expand our understanding of the universe.

In 2021, I graduated from TFO-Bruneck (High School) in Bruneck, Italy, with a specialization in electronics and automation engineering, achieving top grades in my A-Levels. I then pursued a bachelor's degree in computer science at the Technical University of Munich (TUM), which I have successfully completed. I am now continuing my studies with a master's degree in computer science at TUM. Some of the work I have done can be found at my university page.

If you have any questions, comments, or just want to chat, feel free to reach out at dev@manuellerchner.de.
